# **App Name**: PulsoASAP

## Core Features:

- Hero Section with Video: Animated video header to capture user attention, displaying office scenes with happy professionals to communicate health and professional environments.
- Benefits Showcase: Displays the benefits for both companies and employees, such as a stronger corporate culture and better emotional balance.
- Product and Service Catalog: Displays a listing of ASAP products and services.
- Testimonials Section: Dedicated area to show user testimonials, building trust and credibility.
- Sponsorship Section: Area to present sponsorship opportunities for companies, promoting a healthy work environment, highlighting cotas like Master, Ouro, and Prata.
- Contact Form with Selection Options: Custom contact form to allow users to choose whether they are individuals seeking mentorship or companies interested in other services/sponsorships, altering the input fields accordingly.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#0E6CB4) to convey trust and professionalism, reflecting the risk management aspect.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E6F3FC), almost white, creating a clean and calming atmosphere.
- Accent color: Golden gradient to signal value, success and premium services.
- Body font: 'Satoshi', H1 size: 92px, H2 size: 36px, H4 size: 24px
- Sticky header for easy navigation, maintaining menu visibility while scrolling.
- Subtle video animations in the hero section to capture user attention, similar to faltaum.com.br.
- Clean, professional icons that are easy to understand, emphasizing key points within sections.
